About Battambang

Battambang is one of Cambodia's most beautiful provinces, famous for the Bamboo Train, Phnom Sampeau, Bat Cave, ancient temples and delicious Khmer food.

Top Tourist Attractions

Bamboo Train

Ride the famous bamboo railway through beautiful countryside.

Phnom Sampeau

A mountain with caves, temples and wonderful viewpoints.

Bat Cave

Every evening millions of bats fly out creating an amazing view.

Banan Temple

An ancient temple located on top of a hill with spectacular scenery.
